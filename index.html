Privacy Policy for SwiftInvoice

Last updated: January 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

User Personal Information:

  • Name and email: Collected via Google login to identify your account.
  • Contacts: Only if you choose to select a client from your device contacts.

Business Information:

  • Business name, address, city, state, postal code, country, country code
  • Phone number
  • Industry and category
  • Company size
  • Business email

Usage Information:

  • Invoices you create and manage in the app
  • App settings such as notification preferences

Files and Storage: The app may read and write files on your device to store invoices and generate PDFs. Permissions used: READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (limited to Android 28 or lower).

Notifications: The app uses POST_NOTIFICATIONS permission to send notifications about invoice status and app updates.

Internet Access: Permission INTERNET is used to sync invoice data, generate PDFs, and optionally send emails or backups if enabled.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify your user account and associate invoices with your profile
  • To create, store, and manage invoices within the app
  • To send notifications about invoice status and app updates
  • To allow you to select clients from your contacts (if permission granted)
  • To generate PDF invoices for sharing or printing
  • To store and optionally backup business information

3. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal or business information to third parties.
  • Your data is stored locally on your device unless you choose to share or backup manually.

4. Data Security

  • We take reasonable measures to protect your data.
  • All PDF invoices, user information, and business data are stored locally on your device.
  • The app does not collect financial data such as credit card numbers.

5. Your Rights

  • You can disable notifications in the app settings.
  • You can delete invoices and business data at any time.
  • You can revoke access to contacts in your device settings.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at your GitHub Pages URL.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.

By using SwiftInvoice, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
