SwiftInvoice - Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data from SwiftInvoice, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us at support@algostack.co with the subject "Delete my account".
  2. Include the email address you used to log in to the app.

Data that will be deleted

- Your account information (name, email)
- All invoices and business data associated with your account
- Any analytics data linked to your account

Data that will be retained

- Any data required by law or accounting regulations will be retained for up to 7 years.

Processing Time

Your account will be fully removed from SwiftInvoice within 30 days of your request.

Additional Information

If you have any questions or need assistance, contact us at support@algostack.co.
